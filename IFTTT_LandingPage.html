You've enabled IFTTT, Now What?

Use easy automations to seamlessly collect and update data from multiple sources.

Find Relevant Applets

Using the data.world gallery on IFTTT, it's easy to find the applets that relate to your needs. Here's how to get started:

  1. Login to IFTTT or create an account if you don’t already have one
  2. Navigate to the data.world gallery on IFTTT and select Connect
  3. Scroll through the gallery and select an applet to enable

Enable Applets

data.world's library of approved applets on IFTTT allows you to automate a number of common actions simply, so you can spend less time updating and more time with your data

  • Follow the applet’s specific instructions to enable it (you can also choose to enable notifications each time the applet runs)

Editing Applet Settings

Once you've enabled an applet, you can edit the settings at any point by:

  1. Navigating to the specific applet
  2. Clicking the gear icon in the top right corner of the applet
  3. Make any changes and select Save at the bottom of the applet

Examples

Here are a few things you can do with IFTTT and data.world:

  • Refresh your data on a regular schedule with the Applet ‘Sync data every day at a specific time’
  • Add and sync files from Dropbox or Google Drive to a data.world dataset
  • Add attachments from any email with ‘#data.world’ in the subject line to a data.world dataset

Or check out one of the many other applets available from data.world.
